Official Description:

"Those bound to the aspect of Time are fighters, full stop. Their lives are often marked by struggle, not so much because fate has it in for them, but because they are fundamentally incapable of just accepting things as they come. They value action over passive acceptance, even if that may not be the wisest or safest choice. Don't try to tell a Time-bound to sit still and look pretty. They are very goal-focused, and tend to value the destination over the journey, and you won't find them making that journey in any traditional sort of way. To quote cheesy posters found on many a guidance counselor's wall-"impossible is just a word". If you need a miracle, they are who you call. At their best, the Time-bound are empathetic and relentless problem-solvers. At their worst they are ruthless, defensive, and impulsive."

Related Aspects

Time's Inverse is Breath , they share near as many themes and concepts as they oppose in the other, making them a complimentary, if volatile, pairing.

Time's Opposite is Space , they are the ultimate antipodes, evenly cancelling eachother out. This leads to a surprisingly stable relationship between them; a pleasant surprise given they must work together to function.

Doom and Time's relationship as mirrors is not necessarily unique, but it is interesting, especially given Doom is also one of Time's Sibling Aspects. They function as "dark mirrors" of one another, sharing a multitude of their themes and subjects, though in slightly altered ways.

As stated above, Doom is one of Time's sibling aspects, the other is Rage . While the relationship between Rage and Time is more difficult to understand than the relationship to Doom , it becomes quite clear once you realize both symbolize cold, hard logic in addition to unstoppable forces / movement.
